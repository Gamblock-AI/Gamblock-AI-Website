## Usage rules

- Keep the navy and cyan visual language for normal product actions.
- Reserve crimson for urgency and critical support.
- Do not add casino chips, slot machines, betting dashboards, or gambling-brand imagery.
- Do not use generated people as team or advisor portraits.
- Keep raw browsing data claims aligned with the workspace privacy invariants.
